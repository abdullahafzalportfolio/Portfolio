Cookie Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

In short: this Site uses no tracking or advertising cookies. It stores one small preference — your light/dark theme choice — in your browser, and nothing else.

What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small text files a website can store in your browser. “Local storage” is a similar browser feature for saving small preferences on your device. Both let a site remember things between visits.

What this Site stores

  • Theme preference — when you switch between light and dark mode, your choice is saved in your browser’s local storage under the key aa-theme. This stays on your device, is never sent to me or any third party, and is only used to remember how you like the Site to look.

That is the only thing stored. This Site sets no advertising cookies, no analytics cookies, and no third-party tracking.

Third-party services

If you click a link to an external service (for example WhatsApp or a live project), that service may set its own cookies under its own policy. I have no control over those and recommend reviewing their policies.

Managing your preferences

You can clear the saved theme preference at any time by clearing your browser’s site data for this Site, or by using your browser’s privacy settings. Doing so simply resets the Site to its default theme; nothing else is affected.

Changes

If I add any service that uses cookies in future (such as analytics or a booking widget), I will update this page and, where required, ask for your consent first.
